Zoom Business Model Canvas - Overview

Zoom Video Communications provides a video-first communications platform that connects people through frictionless video, voice, chat, and content sharing.

SaaS Platform

Founded in 2011 by Eric Yuan, Zoom became essential during the COVID-19 pandemic, growing from 10 million daily meeting participants in December 2019 to over 300 million by April 2020.

Key Activities

The most important activities Zoom performs to make its business model work.

Core Operations
  • Platform Development: Continuous improvement of video, audio, and collaboration features
  • Infrastructure Management: Maintaining global data centers for reliable service
  • Customer Support: Providing help through various channels (chat, phone, community)
  • Sales & Marketing: Acquiring new customers and retaining existing ones
  • Security & Compliance: Ensuring data protection and meeting regulatory requirements
Key Insight: Zoom's focus on a simple, reliable user experience has been central to its success against established competitors.
